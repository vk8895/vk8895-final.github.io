Turbocharge your Returns


The purpose of this site is to begin turbocharging your wealth! But this starts by first learning how to build wealth. Since the Great Depression, the stock market has proven to be a great wealth building tool, returning an annual average of ~9%. This means, an investor can double their original investment every 8 years!

Imagine you just joined the workforce and put $1,000 in a 401k target-date fund in 2020. By 2060, after a 40-year working career, that original $1,000, per historical performance, would become $32,000, a 32x return! This is great to start. But to really build super-villain like wealth, you need to start turbo-charging your returns. Hence, we will explore how to make additional returns through short-term stock trading.
